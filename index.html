Rip Up Your Nightmares

Generative AI-Powered Image Rehearsal Therapy (IRT) for Nightmare

Use our system!

Say goodbye to nightmares and hello to restful rejuvenation

Nightmares can haunt our sleep, leaving us restless and anxious. But fear not! We bring you an solution that harnesses the generative AI. Our IRT system empowers you to take control of your dreams and transform them into positive, soothing experiences.

With our interactive software, you can reimagine and rehearse your nightmares, turning them into scenarios of strength, resilience, and triumph. Through customizable dream simulations and vivid visualizations, our AI-powered system helps you desensitize and reframe the negative emotions associated with your nightmares.

Step1: Nightmare Description Extraction

In this initial step, the user shares and writes their nightmare experience. The system employs ChatGPT to extract and analyze the key elements of the description, such as themes, emotions, characters, and settings. By accurately capturing the essence of the nightmare, this step lays the foundation for subsequent processing.

Step2: Text-to-Image Generation

Using text-to-image generation model, the system takes the extracted nightmare description and translates it into vivid visual representations. The system generates realistic and detailed images that closely align with the user's description. This transformation from text to visual imagery brings the nightmare to life in a tangible and visually accessible form.

Step3: Generative Image Rehearsal

In this pivotal step, users actively engage with and modify the generated nightmare images. They can interact with, reshape, and introduce positive elements into the visuals, fostering empowering narratives. Through this rehearsal process, users confront and reframe their nightmares, transforming them into more manageable and less distressing experiences.
