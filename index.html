Nightmares can haunt our sleep, leaving us restless and anxious. But fear not! We bring you an solution that harnesses the generative AI. Our IRT system empowers you to take control of your dreams and transform them into positive, soothing experiences.

With our interactive software, you can reimagine and rehearse your nightmares, turning them into scenarios of strength, resilience, and triumph. Step1: Nightmare Description Extraction

In this initial step, the user shares and writes their nightmare experience. The system employs ChatGPT to extract and analyze the key elements of the description, such as themes, emotions, characters, and settings. By accurately capturing the essence of the nightmare, this step lays the foundation for subsequent processing.

Step2: Text-to-Image Generation

Using text-to-image generation model, the system takes the extracted nightmare description and translates it into vivid visual representations. The system generates realistic and detailed images that closely align with the user's description. This transformation from text to visual imagery brings the nightmare to life in a tangible and visually accessible form.

Step3: Generative Image Rehearsal

In this pivotal step, users actively engage with and modify the generated nightmare images. They can interact with, reshape, and introduce positive elements into the visuals, fostering empowering narratives. Through this rehearsal process, users confront and reframe their nightmares, transforming them into more manageable and less distressing experiences.

DreamViz: Dream Visualization and Sharing with AI Generated Videos

Dreams are probes into our cognition, supporting the neural processing of emotional and vital memories during sleep. Sharing dreams has been shown to benefit relationships, such as improving intimacy and empathy regarding the life circumstances of the dreamer. However, the typical format of text-based dream reports limits dream sharing, as the reports can be vague and non-continuous, making dream reports incomprehensible and boring. In this paper, we present DreamViz, a system that turns a text description of a dream or personal experience into a video. It involves decoding information (i.e., what are the characters and scenes) from the text-based dream report with the help of the user and encoding them into a custom AI-generated video.

EmoGarden: Exploring AI-Enabled Visual Journaling for Mental Health

Journaling enhances mental health by allowing individuals to express emotions, reflect on experiences, and become more self-aware. Traditional journaling can be repetitive, but digital platforms provide innovative and engaging options. However, most digital tools are limited to a single mode of expression, hindering engagement and reflection. We propose EmoGarden, a new journaling system that combines text and images to stimulate unique cognitive processes and encourage self-reflection. EmoGarden collaborates with users to generate personalized pictures and scenarios based on their journaling content. Furthermore, it allows users to review and modify past entries for additional reflection and personal growth.
